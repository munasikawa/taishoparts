ABOUT US

We are Taisho Parts & Accessories

Taisho Parts & Accessories Ltd, an automotive parts dealer, was established in February 1990 when the founders identified a gap in the market for new automotive spare parts. Taisho began with a staff of 5 and has since grown to 85 employees, becoming a key player in the rapidly evolving automotive spare parts industry.

In 2009, the founder's older son joined the business and ultimately grew the business by opening a new branch in October 2021; selling Ex Japan parts. Both branches are located on Chogoria Road.

We specialize in genuine new and Ex Japan spare parts including the world famous brands; Depo, Casp, HLL.
