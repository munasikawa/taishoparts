We are Taisho Parts & Accessories

Taisho Parts & Accessories Ltd (TPA) always provides the best quality vehicle car spare parts at the best prices. We specialize in Genuine Spare parts, the world's famous Depo, Casp, Hll & many other brands + best quality after-market parts too! You can't go wrong with us!

Taisho was founded in February 1990. Located at the end of Chogoria Rd

Mission

To provide our consumers safe, good quality products at affordable prices through ensuring safety and durability, and provide affordable solutions through ground-breaking manufacturing channels and leading innovation in our distribution networks.

We aim at creating the ultimate experience for their customers through continuous efforts by achieving day-to-day perfectionism.

Vision

As an adaptive digital technology-driven company, we're constantly improving the way drivers shop for the car parts they need. After being in business for over 25 years—our identity today reflects how we've refocused and streamlined our efforts to build a seamless supply-to-consumer shopping experience for everyday drivers!

With brand and factory-direct self-distribution shipping, we offer a wide selection of quality auto parts for car repair, maintenance, and collision as well as accessories, and our catalog includes over 10,000 products—all at extremely competitive prices. We even offer country-wide delivery, ensuring that drivers can get what they need when they need it to get back on the road quickly.

Finally, we take pride in the team that keeps us running. Together, we achieved remarkable milestones and most importantly, strengthening our commitment to our customers by continuing to transform the way drivers shop for car parts.
